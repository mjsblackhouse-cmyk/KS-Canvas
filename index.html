    Tensions and Trade‑offs

    Excellence–Acceptance

    Pursuit of excellence coexists with acceptance when persistence is no longer productive. Supports sustainability but may conflict with high achievement standards.

    Conviction–Openness

    Strong moral direction coexists with openness to other views. Clarity needs to be maintained while remaining receptive.

    Drive–Pace

    Deliberate moderation of natural intensity prevents burnout but may appear as variable pacing to others.

    Truth–Relationships

    Direct communication supports transparency but can strain relationships when gentler approaches are preferred.
